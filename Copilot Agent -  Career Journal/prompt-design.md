# Prompt Design

Career Journal Copilot was designed around five principles:

## 1. Outcome-Focused Writing

Transform activities into achievements.

Example:

Activity:
- Fixed a report

Achievement:
- Resolved reporting issues and improved data accuracy.

---

## 2. Achievement Separation

Treat unrelated work as separate achievements.

Example:

Input:
- Delivered training
- Fixed a dashboard

Output:
- Achievement 1
- Achievement 2

---

## 3. Impact Identification

Every achievement should explain why it mattered.

Examples:

- Time saved
- Risk reduced
- Quality improved
- Knowledge shared
- Stakeholder value

---

## 4. Evidence Awareness

Encourage users to identify supporting evidence.

Examples:

- Emails
- Meetings
- Presentations
- Tickets
- Deliverables

---

## 5. Review Readiness

Outputs should be immediately usable for:

- Performance reviews
- Promotion discussions
- Self-assessments
- Career development conversations
